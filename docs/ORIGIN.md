# Origin

Campaign governance is not just a naming problem. It is an attribution, audience, and launch problem. The moment a taxonomy changes, teams need a surface that maps naming drift to CRM logic, dashboard continuity, and the next safe move.

We built `campaign-taxonomy-governor` to make that operating layer explicit. The point is not to replace a CRM or ad platform. The point is to show what a MarTech / AdTech operator surface should look like when the audience needs to manage taxonomy pressure, downstream dependencies, and launch posture with real revenue consequences attached.
